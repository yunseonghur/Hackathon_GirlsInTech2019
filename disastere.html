Earthquake


Step 1: REMAIN calm, If inside, stay inside and don't try run .

Step 2: DROP, COVER, AND HOLD Drop under sturdy furniture. Cover as much of your head and torso as you can.Hold ontothe furniture. If you cannot get under sturdy furniture,move to an insidewall or sturdy archway and sitwith your back to thewall, bring your knees to your chest and cover your headwith your arms.

Step 3: STAY away from mirrors and windows.

Step 4: DO NOT exit the building during the shaking.

Step 5: IF OUTDOORS move to an open area away from all structures, especially buildings, bridges, and overhead power lines.Watch for falling debris.

Step 6: IF DRIVING stop in an open area away from all structures especially bridges, overpasses,tunnels, and overhead power lines. Stay as low as possible inside the vehicle.
