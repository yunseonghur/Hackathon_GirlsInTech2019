We decided to make this game so that we may bring awareness to the issue of disaster preparedness (or the lack thereof) and how people are affected by it both here in North America and in developing countries.

Disasterscape

You woke up in your camping bedroom and felt something wrong, so you decided to pick something in your backpack to get out of the room.

When you walked out the camping room, you saw some smoke and smelled somthing bad.

Son of a motherless goat! Fire is coming and there is a tree blocking our way. Did we put an axe in the backpack?

This could be a life-or-death decision... go up or go down?

Ha Ha Ha! You died!

Do you want to try it again?

Thank god, we found our car! We can get to safety now.

We hope that by making this game, we will teach people how to properly respond in the event of a natural disaster.

We hope that by playing our game, people will become aware of the struggle that others face in the event of natural disasters, especially in developing countries. We wish to inspire people to contribute to the effort of disaster relief and recovery however they are willing.
