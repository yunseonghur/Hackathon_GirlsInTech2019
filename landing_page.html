We decided to make this game so that we may bring awareness to the issue of disaster preparedness and how people are affected by it.

Diasterscape




PLAY

We hope that by making this game, we will teach people how to properly respond in the event of a natural disaster.

We hope that by playing our game, people will become aware of the struggle that others face in the event of natural disasters, especially in developing countries. Through this awareness, we wish to inspire people to contribute to the effort of disaster relief and recovery however they are willing.
